Saint Petersburg


Customs
  • Before making arrangements to visit, you must not only obtain a visa, but
       must register it when you have arrived in Saint Petersburg.

  • In Saint Petersburg, tourists will pay a higher entrance fee to get into many
       of the city's attractions while locals will pay a lower entrance fee.

  • ATMs are omnipresent throughout Saint Petersburg, and most establishments
       accept card as a form of payment. Inform your bank that you will be abroad.

  • Similar to Buenos Aires, you must give up your train, bus, or metro seat to the
       elderly, pregnant women, and handicapped passengers if there are none.

  • When meeting male strangers, it is common to give a firm handshake with
       direct eye contact. Avoid a firm shake when meeting women.

  • Upon an invitation into someone's home, men are expected to bring flowers.

  • When drinking alcoholic beverages of any type, place empty bottles on the
       floor as this superstition holds importance amongst some Russians.

    • Faux Pas
  • As mentioned before, some Russians are superstitious. Avoid shaking
       hands over door thresholds, and do not give flowers in even numbers.

  • Never wear shoes inside someone's home as Russians usually wear
       indoor slippers when inside.

  • Do not enter a Russian Orthodox Church without covering your head if
       you are a woman. Men must remove their hats upon entering.

  • Last, but certainly not least, topics involving international politics
       pertaining to Russia should be kept away from. When asking questions
       about the country, do so without revealing your opinion or being
       argumentative.

    • Safety
  • Saint Petersburg ranks nearly the same as Bergen, Norway and Berlin,
       Germany in terms of crime and safety index scores meaning that it is
       just as safe as the average European city.

  • Like almost any large city, be alert when in a crowded area as pickpockets do
       occur in Saint Petersburg. Children are sometimes used to perform thefts, so
       even if an innocent looking child comes close to you, be observant of your
       surroundings.

  • Do not get intoxicated with individuals who you don't know.
